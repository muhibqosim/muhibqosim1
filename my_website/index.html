On humanitarian outreach to an IDP camp in Kano, Nigeria

On humanitarian outreach to an IDP camp in Kano, Nigeria

The curiosity to know more about the plight of IDP came about while I was involved in a doctrinal research on the violation of the internally displaced persons human rights in the fight against boko haram in the north-eastern region of nigeria sometimes in 2022. That being my first time of reading so much on and, conducting research on the ...... about these groups of persons who have been forced to flee their homes & places of habitual residence as a result of or in order to avoid the effects of armed conflict.

I was privilege to participate in the humanitarian outreach pioneered by the Bayero University Law Clinic in conjunction with CELOHA & JCI, BUK. I so much felt about the hardships these persons are going through. To be forced to live one's place of abode as a result of conflict and to ensure safety of one's life is not so easy.
